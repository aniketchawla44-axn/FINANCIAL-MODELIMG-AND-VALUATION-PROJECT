# FINANCIAL-MODELIMG-AND-VALUATION-PROJECT
📂 Project Overview
1. 🎨 Asian Paints: DCF Valuation Report
An intrinsic valuation of Asian Paints Ltd. to determine its fair market value based on its free cash flows and risk parameters.

Historical Analysis: 5-year historical financial statement scrubbing and normalized margin analysis.

WACC & Beta: Calculated Cost of Equity using Regression Beta against Nifty 50 and unlevered/re-levered industry Beta.

Free Cash Flow to Firm (FCFF): Projected future unlevered free cash flows based on revenue drivers.

Terminal Value & Sensitivity: Estimated using the Perpetual Growth Method & Exit Multiple approaches, backed by sensitivity tables (WACC vs. Terminal Growth).

2. 🚗 Tata Motors: Three-Statement Financial Model
A fully dynamic, driver-based 3-statement integrated financial model for Tata Motors Ltd.

Integrated Framework: Seamless linkage between the Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement.

Revenue Drivers: Segment-level forecasting (JLR, Commercial Vehicles, and Passenger Vehicles/EVs).

Working Capital & Capex Schedules: Driver-based projections for inventory days, receivable days, payable days, and depreciation schedules.

Debt & Interest Scenarios: Dynamic schedules calculating revolving debt and interest payouts.

🛠️ Skills & Tools Demonstrated
Financial Modeling & Forecasting: Revenue drivers, expense ratios, and asset/liability forecasting.

Business Valuation: DCF (FCFF/FCFE), WACC calculation, terminal value modeling.

Advanced MS Excel: Index/Match, XLOOKUP, Data Tables (Sensitivity analysis), Dynamic charts, and Scenario Manager.

Financial Analysis: DuPont analysis, margin expansion/contraction analysis, leverage, and liquidity ratios.
